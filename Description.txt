TabZapDups automatically closes duplicate tabs to keep your browser clean and organized.

When you open a link that's already open in another tab, TabZapDups detects the duplicate and closes it after a brief delay. Click the extension icon to cancel if needed.

Features:
- Automatic duplicate tab detection
- 5-second delay before closing (cancel anytime)
- Customizable URL matching patterns
- Ignore patterns for specific sites
- Settings sync across devices
